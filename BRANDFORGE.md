**BrandForge**: Complete brand strategy ecosystem for design thinking teams

## Overview

BrandForge is a comprehensive brand strategy framework that provides intelligent orchestration of brand development, creative direction, user research, and market analysis. Built for world-class design thinking teams, it transforms brand development from a fragmented process into a cohesive, research-driven ecosystem.

## Core Differentiator

Unlike traditional brand development tools, BrandForge includes **Dynamic Persona Technology** - the ability to generate unlimited, realistic synthetic users for research validation, eliminating the constraints of traditional user recruitment while maintaining research rigor.

## Quick Start

```bash
# Initialize a brand project
/brandforge init --project "[project-name]" --type [startup|enterprise|rebrand]

# Complete brand development workflow
/brandforge forge --from-strategy-to-launch --market-research --user-validation

# Synthetic user research
/brandforge research --synthetic-users --demographic "[target-audience]" --count 50
```

## Architecture Overview

BrandForge follows proven persona-based architecture patterns with specialized adaptations for brand strategy:

1. **Persona-Driven**: 12 specialized personas for brand strategy domains
2. **Command-Centric**: Intuitive command system for brand workflows
3. **Flag-Optimized**: Precision targeting for demographics and research
4. **MCP-Enhanced**: Integration with market research and creative tools
5. **Quality-Gated**: 8-step validation for brand deliverables
6. **Wave-Enabled**: Complex orchestration for enterprise projects
