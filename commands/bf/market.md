# /brandforge market - Market Intelligence & Analysis

## Command Structure
```yaml
---
command: "/brandforge market"
category: "Market Intelligence & Analysis"
purpose: "Comprehensive market analysis, sizing, and opportunity assessment"
wave-enabled: true
performance-profile: "complex"
---
```

## Purpose
Advanced market intelligence that provides comprehensive market analysis, opportunity sizing, competitive positioning, and growth strategy development based on evidence-based market insights.

## Usage Patterns
```bash
/brandforge market [industry] --analysis [type]
/brandforge market --sizing --opportunities --trends
/brandforge market --competitive --positioning --strategy
/brandforge market --forecasting --scenarios --planning
```

## Arguments
- **industry**: Target industry or market sector
- **analysis**: Type of market analysis to perform

## Market Analysis Types
- **comprehensive**: Full market analysis and intelligence
- **sizing**: Market size and opportunity assessment
- **competitive**: Competitive landscape analysis
- **trends**: Market trends and forecasting
- **opportunities**: Market opportunity identification
- **positioning**: Market positioning analysis
- **forecasting**: Future market predictions
- **segmentation**: Market segmentation analysis

## Flags
- `--sizing`: Market size and growth analysis
- `--opportunities`: Market opportunity identification
- `--trends`: Trend analysis and forecasting
- `--competitive`: Competitive landscape analysis
- `--positioning`: Market positioning assessment
- `--forecasting`: Future market predictions
- `--scenarios`: Multiple future scenarios
- `--segmentation`: Market segmentation analysis
- `--pricing`: Market pricing analysis
- `--channels**: Distribution channel analysis
- `--barriers**: Market entry barriers
- `--regulations**: Regulatory environment analysis

## Auto-Persona Activation
- **analyst**: Market intelligence and competitive analysis
- **architect**: Market strategy and positioning
- **performance**: Market efficiency and optimization
- **mentor**: Market analysis education and best practices

## MCP Server Integration
- **Sequential**: Multi-dimensional market analysis
- **Context7**: Market analysis frameworks and methodologies
- **Magic**: Market visualization and competitive mapping

## Market Analysis Framework

### Market Sizing
- **TAM Analysis**: Total Addressable Market calculation
- **SAM Definition**: Serviceable Available Market identification
- **SOM Estimation**: Serviceable Obtainable Market sizing
- **Growth Projections**: Market growth rate predictions
- **Revenue Potential**: Revenue opportunity assessment
- **Market Share**: Potential market share calculations

### Competitive Intelligence
- **Competitor Mapping**: Competitive landscape visualization
- **Market Share Analysis**: Current market share distribution
- **Competitive Positioning**: Relative competitive positioning
- **SWOT Analysis**: Strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, threats
- **Benchmarking**: Performance comparison metrics
- **Competitive Advantages**: Sustainable competitive advantages

### Trend Analysis
- **Market Trends**: Current and emerging market trends
- **Technology Trends**: Technology adoption and impact
- **Consumer Trends**: Changing consumer behaviors and preferences
- **Regulatory Trends**: Regulatory environment changes
- **Economic Trends**: Economic factors affecting the market
- **Social Trends**: Social and cultural trend impacts

### Opportunity Assessment
- **Market Gaps**: Unmet customer needs and market gaps
- **White Space**: Underserved market segments
- **Innovation Opportunities**: Innovation and disruption opportunities
- **Partnership Opportunities**: Strategic partnership potential
- **Geographic Expansion**: Geographic market expansion opportunities
- **Vertical Expansion**: Industry vertical expansion opportunities

## Quality Gates
- **Data Accuracy**: Accurate and reliable market data
- **Analysis Rigor**: Methodologically sound analysis
- **Source Credibility**: Credible and authoritative data sources
- **Validation**: Independent validation of findings
- **Actionability**: Practical and actionable insights
- **Timeliness**: Current and relevant market information

## Examples
```bash
# Comprehensive market analysis
/brandforge market "SaaS Healthcare" --comprehensive --sizing --opportunities

# Competitive landscape analysis
/brandforge market --competitive --positioning --strategy

# Market opportunity identification
/brandforge market --opportunities --trends --forecasting

# Market entry strategy
/brandforge market --barriers --channels --regulations --strategy

# Future market scenarios
/brandforge market --scenarios --forecasting --planning
```
