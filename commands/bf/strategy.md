# /brandforge strategy - Strategic Planning & Positioning

## Command Structure
```yaml
---
command: "/brandforge strategy"
category: "Brand Strategy Development"
purpose: "Strategic brand positioning and competitive differentiation"
wave-enabled: true
performance-profile: "complex"
---
```

## Purpose
Comprehensive strategic planning that defines brand positioning, competitive differentiation, market entry strategy, and long-term brand architecture. Creates actionable strategic frameworks with measurable outcomes.

## Usage Patterns
```bash
/brandforge strategy [brand-name] --positioning [framework]
/brandforge strategy --competitive-analysis --differentiation
/brandforge strategy --market-entry --go-to-market
/brandforge strategy --architecture --scalability
```

## Arguments
- **brand-name**: Target brand for strategic development
- **positioning**: Core positioning framework or methodology

## Strategic Frameworks
- **blue-ocean**: Uncontested market space creation
- **value-proposition**: Customer value-focused positioning
- **category-creation**: New market category definition
- **competitive-differentiation**: Direct competitive positioning
- **disruption**: Market disruption strategy
- **premium-positioning**: Luxury/premium market strategy
- **mass-market**: Broad market appeal strategy

## Flags
- `--positioning [framework]`: Select strategic positioning approach
- `--competitive-analysis`: Deep competitive landscape analysis
- `--differentiation`: Unique value proposition development
- `--market-entry`: Go-to-market strategy design
- `--architecture`: Brand architecture and hierarchy
- `--scalability`: Long-term growth strategy
- `--validation`: Strategy validation and testing
- `--metrics`: Success metrics and KPI definition
- `--scenario-planning`: Multiple future scenarios
- `--risk-assessment`: Strategic risk analysis

## Auto-Persona Activation
- **architect**: Strategic architecture and systems thinking
- **analyst**: Market analysis and competitive intelligence
- **mentor**: Strategic guidance and best practices
- **performance**: Strategic performance optimization

## MCP Server Integration
- **Sequential**: Multi-dimensional strategic analysis
- **Context7**: Strategic frameworks and methodologies
- **Magic**: Strategic visual frameworks and positioning maps

## Strategic Analysis Framework

### Market Analysis
- **TAM/SAM/SOM**: Total addressable market sizing
- **Competitive Landscape**: Direct and indirect competitor analysis
- **Customer Segmentation**: Detailed audience persona development
- **Trend Analysis**: Market trends and future predictions
- **Barrier Analysis**: Entry barriers and competitive moats

### Positioning Strategy
- **Value Proposition**: Core customer value definition
- **Brand Promise**: Commitment to customers
- **Brand Personality**: Human characteristics and tone
- **Brand Essence**: Core brand identity
- **Positioning Statement**: Clear market positioning

### Differentiation Strategy
- **Unique Value Proposition**: Competitive advantages
- **Brand Differentiators**: Distinctive brand elements
- **Competitive Advantages**: Sustainable advantages
- **Brand Positioning Map**: Visual competitive positioning
- **Differentiation Metrics**: Measurable differentiation factors

## Quality Gates
- **Market Validation**: Strategy validation with target audience
- **Competitive Testing**: Differentiation effectiveness testing
- **Feasibility Assessment**: Implementation feasibility analysis
- **Risk Evaluation**: Strategic risk assessment and mitigation
- **Success Metrics**: Measurable strategic outcomes defined

## Examples
```bash
# Blue ocean strategy development
/brandforge strategy "TechFlow" --positioning blue-ocean

# Competitive differentiation analysis
/brandforge strategy --competitive-analysis --differentiation

# Market entry strategy
/brandforge strategy --market-entry --go-to-market --validation

# Enterprise brand architecture
/brandforge strategy --architecture --scalability --enterprise

# Scenario planning for growth
/brandforge strategy --scenario-planning --risk-assessment
```
