# /brandforge research - User & Market Research

## Command Structure
```yaml
---
command: "/brandforge research"
category: "Brand Research & Intelligence"
purpose: "Comprehensive user research and market intelligence gathering"
wave-enabled: true
performance-profile: "complex"
---
```

## Purpose
Systematic research methodology that combines user research, market analysis, competitive intelligence, and cultural insights to inform brand strategy with evidence-based insights.

## Usage Patterns
```bash
/brandforge research [topic] --method [approach]
/brandforge research --user-personas --market-analysis
/brandforge research --competitive-intelligence --trends
/brandforge research --validation --testing --insights
```

## Arguments
- **topic**: Research topic or brand focus area
- **method**: Research methodology or approach

## Research Methods
- **qualitative**: In-depth interviews and observations
- **quantitative**: Surveys and statistical analysis
- **mixed-methods**: Combined qualitative and quantitative
- **ethnographic**: Cultural and behavioral insights
- **competitive**: Competitor analysis and benchmarking
- **market-sizing**: Market opportunity assessment
- **user-journey**: Customer experience mapping
- **sentiment**: Brand sentiment and perception analysis

## Flags
- `--user-personas`: Detailed customer persona development
- `--market-analysis`: Comprehensive market research
- `--competitive-intelligence`: Competitor analysis and insights
- `--trends`: Trend analysis and forecasting
- `--validation`: Research validation and testing
- `--testing`: User testing and feedback collection
- `--insights`: Insight generation and synthesis
- `--cultural`: Cultural and demographic insights
- `--behavioral**: Behavioral psychology insights
- `--segmentation**: Market segmentation analysis

## Auto-Persona Activation
- **analyst**: Research analysis and insight generation
- **mentor**: Research methodology and best practices
- **qa**: Research quality and validation
- **performance**: Research efficiency and optimization

## MCP Server Integration
- **Sequential**: Multi-dimensional research analysis
- **Context7**: Research methodologies and frameworks
- **Playwright**: User testing and validation

## Research Framework

### User Research
- **Persona Development**: Detailed customer archetypes
- **Journey Mapping**: Customer experience mapping
- **Pain Point Analysis**: Customer challenges and needs
- **Motivation Research**: Customer drivers and goals
- **Behavioral Analysis**: Usage patterns and preferences
- **Satisfaction Metrics**: Customer satisfaction and loyalty

### Market Research
- **Market Sizing**: Total addressable market analysis
- **Growth Trends**: Market growth and opportunity assessment
- **Competitive Landscape**: Direct and indirect competitors
- **Market Gaps**: Unmet customer needs and opportunities
- **Pricing Research**: Optimal pricing strategies
- **Distribution Channels**: Market access and reach strategies

### Competitive Intelligence
- **Competitor Analysis**: Strengths, weaknesses, positioning
- **Benchmarking**: Performance comparison and metrics
- **Differentiation Analysis**: Unique value proposition gaps
- **Market Share**: Competitive positioning and opportunities
- **Innovation Tracking**: Competitor innovation and trends
- **Customer Switching**: Customer acquisition and retention insights

### Cultural Insights
- **Demographic Analysis**: Target audience characteristics
- **Psychographic Analysis**: Values, attitudes, and lifestyle
- **Cultural Trends**: Cultural shifts and influences
- **Regional Insights**: Geographic market differences
- **Generational Analysis**: Age cohort characteristics
- **Social Influences**: Community and social factors

## Quality Gates
- **Research Validity**: Methodological rigor and validity
- **Sample Representativeness**: Representative target audience
- **Data Quality**: Accurate and reliable data collection
- **Insight Actionability**: Practical and applicable insights
- **Bias Mitigation**: Research bias identification and mitigation
- **Ethical Compliance**: Research ethics and privacy compliance

## Examples
```bash
# Comprehensive user research
/brandforge research --user-personas --market-analysis --insights

# Competitive intelligence gathering
/brandforge research --competitive-intelligence --benchmarking --trends

# Market validation research
/brandforge research --validation --testing --segmentation

# Cultural insights for new market
/brandforge research --cultural --behavioral --demographic

# Customer journey research
/brandforge research --user-journey --pain-points --satisfaction
```
