# /brandforge content - Content Strategy & Creation

## Command Structure
```yaml
---
command: "/brandforge content"
category: "Brand Content Development"
purpose: "Comprehensive content strategy, creation, and brand storytelling"
wave-enabled: true
performance-profile: "optimization"
---
```

## Purpose
Strategic content development that creates compelling brand narratives, content strategies, and multi-channel content assets that align with brand positioning and drive audience engagement.

## Usage Patterns
```bash
/brandforge content [brand-name] --strategy [approach]
/brandforge content --narrative --storytelling --voice
/brandforge content --calendar --creation --distribution
/brandforge content --optimization --performance --analytics
```

## Arguments
- **brand-name**: Target brand for content development
- **strategy**: Content strategy approach or methodology

## Content Strategy Approaches
- **storytelling**: Narrative-driven brand storytelling
- **educational**: Educational and thought leadership content
- **entertainment**: Engaging and entertaining content
- **inspirational**: Motivational and aspirational content
- **transactional**: Conversion-focused content
- **community**: Community-building and engagement content
- **authority**: Expertise and authority-building content

## Flags
- `--strategy`: Content strategy development
- `--narrative`: Brand storytelling and narrative development
- `--voice`: Brand voice and tone definition
- `--calendar`: Content calendar and planning
- `--creation`: Content creation and production
- `--distribution`: Content distribution strategy
- `--optimization`: Content optimization and performance
- `--storytelling`: Brand story development
- `--messaging`: Core messaging framework
- `--channels`: Multi-channel content strategy
- `--seo`: Search-optimized content strategy
- `--analytics`: Content performance analytics

## Auto-Persona Activation
- **scribe**: Content creation and brand storytelling
- **mentor**: Content strategy education and best practices
- **analyst**: Content performance and optimization
- **frontend**: Content user experience and engagement

## MCP Server Integration
- **Sequential**: Multi-channel content strategy development
- **Context7**: Content strategy frameworks and methodologies
- **Magic**: Visual content creation and brand storytelling

## Content Strategy Framework

### Brand Storytelling
- **Brand Narrative**: Core brand story and messaging
- **Origin Story**: Brand founding and mission story
- **Customer Stories**: Customer success and transformation stories
- **Founder Story**: Leadership and vision storytelling
- **Values Story**: Brand values and purpose storytelling
- **Future Story**: Vision and aspiration storytelling

### Content Pillars
- **Educational Content**: Thought leadership and expertise sharing
- **Entertainment Content**: Engaging and shareable content
- **Inspirational Content**: Motivational and aspirational messaging
- **Community Content**: User-generated and community-focused content
- **Product Content**: Product information and benefits content
- **Behind-the-Scenes**: Authentic brand transparency content

### Content Calendar
- **Editorial Calendar**: Planned content topics and timing
- **Seasonal Content**: Timely and relevant seasonal content
- **Campaign Content**: Integrated campaign content planning
- **Evergreen Content**: Timeless and continuously relevant content
- **Trending Content**: Real-time trending topic content
- **User-Generated**: Community and customer-created content

### Multi-Channel Strategy
- **Website Content**: Homepage, about, product, and blog content
- **Social Media**: Platform-specific content for each channel
- **Email Marketing**: Newsletter and promotional email content
- **Video Content**: Brand videos, tutorials, and testimonials
- **Podcast Content**: Audio content and interviews
- **Interactive Content**: Quizzes, calculators, and tools

### Content Optimization
- **SEO Optimization**: Search engine optimization for discoverability
- **Performance Analytics**: Content performance measurement
- **A/B Testing**: Content testing and optimization
- **Engagement Metrics**: Audience engagement and interaction tracking
- **Conversion Optimization**: Content-driven conversion improvement
- **Personalization**: Personalized content experiences

## Quality Gates
- **Brand Alignment**: Content aligns with brand strategy and positioning
- **Audience Relevance**: Content resonates with target audience
- **Engagement Quality**: High-quality audience engagement and interaction
- **Performance Metrics**: Measurable content performance outcomes
- **Consistency**: Consistent brand voice and messaging across channels
- **SEO Effectiveness**: Search-optimized content performance
