# /brandforge analyze - Brand Analysis & Insights

## Command Structure
```yaml
---
command: "/brandforge analyze"
category: "Brand Analysis & Intelligence"
purpose: "Comprehensive brand analysis, competitive intelligence, and strategic insights"
wave-enabled: true
performance-profile: "complex"
---
```

## Purpose
Advanced brand analysis that provides deep insights into brand performance, competitive positioning, market dynamics, and strategic opportunities through evidence-based analytical frameworks and actionable recommendations.

## Usage Patterns
```bash
/brandforge analyze [brand-scope] --focus [area]
/brandforge analyze --comprehensive --competitive --insights
/brandforge analyze --performance --metrics --optimization
/brandforge analyze --market-position --opportunities --strategy
```

## Arguments
- **brand-scope**: Scope of brand analysis (brand-name|market|competitive)
- **focus**: Specific analysis focus area

## Analysis Focus Areas
- **comprehensive**: Complete brand analysis across all dimensions
- **competitive**: Competitive landscape and positioning analysis
- **performance**: Brand performance and effectiveness analysis
- **positioning**: Market positioning and differentiation analysis
- **customer**: Customer perception and behavior analysis
- **financial**: Brand financial performance and ROI analysis
- **digital**: Digital presence and online performance analysis
- **sentiment**: Brand sentiment and reputation analysis

## Flags
- `--comprehensive`: Multi-dimensional brand analysis
- `--competitive`: Competitive intelligence and benchmarking
- `--performance`: Performance measurement and analytics
- `--positioning`: Market positioning and differentiation
- `--customer`: Customer insights and behavior analysis
- `--financial`: Financial performance and ROI analysis
- `--digital`: Digital presence and online analysis
- `--sentiment`: Brand sentiment and reputation analysis
- `--trends`: Trend analysis and future insights
- `--benchmarking`: Industry benchmarking and best practices
- `--optimization`: Optimization opportunities and recommendations
- `--forecasting`: Future performance predictions and scenarios

## Auto-Persona Activation
- **analyzer**: Deep analytical insights and root cause analysis
- **architect**: Strategic analysis and system-level insights
- **performance**: Performance analysis and optimization
- **mentor**: Analytical insights and strategic guidance

## MCP Server Integration
- **Sequential**: Multi-dimensional analytical framework
- **Context7**: Analytical methodologies and competitive intelligence
- **Playwright**: Digital presence analysis and user behavior insights

## Analytical Framework

### Brand Performance Analysis
- **Brand Health**: Overall brand health and vitality assessment
- **Brand Equity**: Brand value and equity measurement
- **Brand Awareness**: Brand recognition and recall metrics
- **Brand Perception**: Customer brand perception and sentiment
- **Brand Loyalty**: Customer loyalty and advocacy measurement
- **Brand Differentiation**: Competitive differentiation analysis

### Competitive Intelligence
- **Competitive Landscape**: Comprehensive competitive environment analysis
- **Market Share**: Relative market position and share analysis
- **Competitive Positioning**: Direct competitor positioning comparison
- **SWOT Analysis**: Strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, threats
- **Benchmarking**: Performance benchmarking against competitors
- **Competitive Advantages**: Sustainable competitive advantage assessment

### Customer Analysis
- **Customer Segmentation**: Detailed customer segment analysis
- **Customer Journey**: End-to-end customer experience mapping
- **Customer Satisfaction**: Customer satisfaction and NPS analysis
- **Customer Behavior**: Purchase behavior and engagement patterns
- **Customer Lifetime Value**: Customer value and retention analysis
- **Customer Acquisition**: Customer acquisition channel effectiveness

### Digital Presence Analysis
- **Website Performance**: Website traffic, engagement, and conversion
- **Social Media Performance**: Social media presence and engagement
- **Search Performance**: SEO performance and search visibility
- **Content Performance**: Content engagement and effectiveness
- **Digital Advertising**: Digital ad performance and ROI
- **Online Reputation**: Online reputation and sentiment analysis

### Financial Analysis
- **Brand ROI**: Return on brand investment measurement
- **Revenue Attribution**: Brand-driven revenue contribution
- **Cost Efficiency**: Brand marketing cost efficiency analysis
- **Growth Metrics**: Brand-driven growth and expansion metrics
- **Market Valuation**: Brand contribution to company valuation
- **Financial Impact**: Overall financial impact of brand initiatives

## Quality Gates
- **Data Accuracy**: Accurate and reliable data sources
- **Analytical Rigor**: Methodologically sound analysis
- **Actionable Insights**: Practical and implementable insights
- **Comprehensive Coverage**: Complete analysis across all relevant dimensions
- **Competitive Context**: Analysis within competitive and market context
- **Future Orientation**: Forward-looking insights and recommendations

## Examples
```bash
# Comprehensive brand analysis
/brandforge analyze --comprehensive --competitive --insights

# Competitive intelligence gathering
/brandforge analyze --competitive --benchmarking --positioning

# Customer behavior analysis
/brandforge analyze --customer --journey --satisfaction

# Digital performance analysis
/brandforge analyze --digital --performance --optimization

# Financial brand impact analysis
/brandforge analyze --financial --roi --growth --forecasting
```
