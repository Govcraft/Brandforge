# /brandforge launch - Launch Orchestration

## Command Structure
```yaml
---
command: "/brandforge launch"
category: "Brand Launch & Go-to-Market"
purpose: "Complete brand launch orchestration and go-to-market strategy"
wave-enabled: true
performance-profile: "optimization"
---
```

## Purpose
Comprehensive launch orchestration that coordinates brand introduction, market entry, customer acquisition, and sustained market presence. Manages launch campaigns, PR strategy, and initial market traction building.

## Usage Patterns
```bash
/brandforge launch [brand-name] --campaign [type]
/brandforge launch --soft-launch --validation --feedback
/brandforge launch --full-launch --marketing --pr
/brandforge launch --scaling --growth --optimization
```

## Arguments
- **brand-name**: Brand to be launched
- **campaign**: Launch campaign type or strategy

## Launch Types
- **soft-launch**: Limited market introduction with validation
- **full-launch**: Complete market introduction with full campaign
- **phased**: Staged launch across markets or segments
- **exclusive**: Premium or invitation-only launch
- **viral**: Social media and viral marketing launch
- **partnership**: Co-branded or partnership launch
- **event**: Event-based launch with PR and media

## Flags
- `--campaign`: Launch campaign strategy and planning
- `--marketing`: Marketing campaign development and execution
- `--pr`: Public relations and media strategy
- `--soft-launch`: Controlled market introduction
- `--validation`: Launch validation and feedback collection
- `--customer-acquisition`: Customer acquisition strategy
- `--scaling`: Post-launch scaling and growth
- `--optimization`: Launch optimization and improvement
- `--channels`: Multi-channel launch strategy
- `--timeline`: Launch timeline and milestone planning
- `--budget`: Launch budget and resource allocation
- `--risk-management`: Launch risk assessment and mitigation
