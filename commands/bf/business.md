# /brandforge business - Business Model Design

## Command Structure
```yaml
---
command: "/brandforge business"
category: "Business Model Development"
purpose: "Business model design, revenue strategy, and brand monetization"
wave-enabled: true
performance-profile: "complex"
---
```

## Purpose
Strategic business model development that aligns brand strategy with revenue generation, pricing strategies, and sustainable business growth. Creates monetization frameworks that support brand positioning and long-term value creation.

## Usage Patterns
```bash
/brandforge business [model-type] --strategy [approach]
/brandforge business --model --revenue --pricing
/brandforge business --validation --testing --optimization
/brandforge business --scaling --growth --expansion
```

## Arguments
- **model-type**: Business model type or framework
- **strategy**: Business strategy approach

## Business Model Types
- **saas**: Software-as-a-Service subscription model
- **marketplace**: Multi-sided platform model
- **freemium**: Free-to-premium conversion model
- **subscription**: Recurring revenue model
- **transaction**: Pay-per-transaction model
- **licensing**: Intellectual property licensing model
- **affiliate**: Commission-based model
- **consulting**: Professional services model

## Flags
- `--model`: Business model canvas development
- `--revenue`: Revenue stream identification and optimization
- `--pricing`: Pricing strategy and testing
- `--validation`: Business model validation
- `--scaling`: Scaling and growth strategy
- `--channels`: Distribution channel strategy
- `--partnerships`: Strategic partnership development
- `--metrics`: Business performance metrics
- `--financial`: Financial modeling and projections
- `--risk`: Business risk assessment

## Auto-Persona Activation
- **architect**: Business architecture and system design
- **analyst**: Business analysis and financial modeling
- **performance**: Business optimization and efficiency
- **mentor**: Business strategy education and guidance

## MCP Server Integration
- **Sequential**: Multi-dimensional business analysis
- **Context7**: Business model frameworks and methodologies
- **Magic**: Business model visualization and strategy mapping

## Business Model Framework

### Value Proposition
- **Customer Segments**: Primary and secondary target customers
- **Value Creation**: Unique value delivered to customers
- **Pain Points**: Customer problems and challenges solved
- **Gain Creators**: Benefits and outcomes delivered
- **Customer Relationships**: How relationships are established and maintained

### Revenue Streams
- **Subscription Revenue**: Recurring subscription income
- **Transaction Revenue**: One-time purchase income
- **Service Revenue**: Professional services income
- **Licensing Revenue**: Intellectual property licensing income
- **Advertising Revenue**: Advertising and sponsorship income
- **Commission Revenue**: Affiliate and commission-based income
- **Data Revenue**: Data monetization opportunities

### Cost Structure
- **Fixed Costs**: Infrastructure and operational costs
- **Variable Costs**: Per-unit production and delivery costs
- **Customer Acquisition**: Marketing and sales costs
- **Customer Retention**: Support and relationship costs
- **Technology Costs**: Platform and development costs
- **Personnel Costs**: Team and operational costs

### Key Resources
- **Intellectual Property**: Brand, patents, and proprietary assets
- **Human Capital**: Team expertise and capabilities
- **Technology**: Platform and infrastructure assets
- **Brand Assets**: Brand recognition and reputation
- **Customer Data**: Customer insights and analytics
- **Partnerships**: Strategic relationships and networks

### Key Activities
- **Product Development**: Product and service development
- **Marketing**: Brand building and customer acquisition
- **Sales**: Customer conversion and relationship building
- **Customer Success**: Customer support and retention
- **Operations**: Service delivery and fulfillment
- **Innovation**: Continuous improvement and innovation

### Key Partnerships
- **Strategic Alliances**: Complementary service partnerships
- **Channel Partners**: Distribution and sales partnerships
- **Technology Partners**: Platform and integration partnerships
- **Marketing Partners**: Co-marketing and promotional partnerships
- **Supplier Partners**: Supply chain and fulfillment partnerships
- **Customer Partners**: Customer referral and advocacy programs

## Quality Gates
- **Market Validation**: Business model validated with target market
- **Financial Viability**: Sustainable and profitable business model
- **Scalability Assessment**: Model scales efficiently with growth
- **Competitive Advantage**: Sustainable competitive advantages
- **Risk Mitigation**: Key business risks identified and addressed
- **Performance Metrics**: Measurable business outcomes defined

## Examples
```bash
# SaaS business model development
/brandforge business saas --model --revenue --pricing

# Marketplace business model
/brandforge business marketplace --validation --scaling --partnerships

# Subscription business optimization
/brandforge business subscription --metrics --financial --optimization

# Consulting business model
/brandforge business consulting --channels --pricing --validation

# Freemium conversion strategy
/brandforge business freemium --revenue --conversion --scaling
```
