# /brandforge task - Project Management

## Command Structure
```yaml
---
command: "/brandforge task"
category: "Brand Project Management"
purpose: "Comprehensive brand project management and task orchestration"
wave-enabled: true
performance-profile: "complex"
---
```

## Purpose
Advanced project management system for complex brand development projects, providing task orchestration, milestone tracking, resource allocation, and cross-functional coordination across extended timelines.

## Usage Patterns
```bash
/brandforge task [operation] [project-name]
/brandforge task create "Brand Redesign" --scope enterprise
/brandforge task plan --phases --milestones --resources
/brandforge task track --progress --analytics --reporting
```

## Arguments
- **operation**: Task management operation (create|plan|track|report)
- **project-name**: Brand project identifier

## Task Operations
- **create**: Create new brand project with structure
- **plan**: Comprehensive project planning and scheduling
- **track**: Progress tracking and milestone management
- **report**: Project reporting and analytics
- **optimize**: Project optimization and resource management
- **collaborate**: Team collaboration and coordination
- **archive**: Project completion and archival

## Flags
- `--create`: Create new brand project structure
- `--plan`: Project planning and timeline development
- `--track`: Progress tracking and milestone management
- `--resources`: Resource allocation and management
- `--milestones`: Milestone definition and tracking
- `--dependencies`: Task dependency management
- `--collaboration`: Team collaboration and communication
- `--reporting`: Project reporting and analytics
- `--risk-management`: Project risk assessment and mitigation
- `--quality-gates`: Quality checkpoint definition
- `--optimization`: Project optimization and efficiency
- `--integration`: Cross-functional integration and coordination

## Project Framework

### Project Structure
- **Epic Level**: Major brand initiatives (3-6 months)
- **Story Level**: Significant deliverables (2-4 weeks)
- **Task Level**: Individual work items (1-5 days)
- **Sub-task Level**: Detailed work breakdown (hours)

### Phase Management
- **Discovery Phase**: Research and analysis
- **Strategy Phase**: Strategic planning and positioning
- **Creative Phase**: Creative development and design
- **Validation Phase**: Testing and validation
- **Implementation Phase**: Asset development and deployment
- **Launch Phase**: Go-to-market and launch execution

### Resource Management
- **Human Resources**: Team allocation and skill matching
- **Budget Allocation**: Financial resource planning and tracking
- **Timeline Management**: Schedule optimization and milestone tracking
- **Quality Assurance**: Continuous quality monitoring and improvement
- **Risk Assessment**: Proactive risk identification and mitigation
- **Stakeholder Management**: Cross-functional coordination and communication

### Progress Tracking
- **Milestone Tracking**: Key deliverable and checkpoint monitoring
- **Progress Analytics**: Real-time progress measurement and reporting
- **Performance Metrics**: Project performance and efficiency measurement
- **Timeline Adherence**: Schedule compliance and adjustment tracking
- **Resource Utilization**: Resource efficiency and optimization
- **Quality Metrics**: Quality assurance and compliance monitoring

## Quality Gates
- **Milestone Completion**: Deliverable quality and completeness validation
- **Timeline Adherence**: Schedule compliance and adjustment management
- **Resource Efficiency**: Optimal resource utilization and allocation
- **Stakeholder Satisfaction**: Cross-functional team satisfaction and alignment
- **Quality Standards**: Consistent quality across all deliverables
- **Risk Mitigation**: Proactive risk management and resolution
