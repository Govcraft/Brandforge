# /brandforge improve - Quality Enhancement

## Command Structure
```yaml
---
command: "/brandforge improve"
category: "Brand Quality Enhancement"
purpose: "Systematic brand improvement, optimization, and quality enhancement"
wave-enabled: true
performance-profile: "optimization"
---
```

## Purpose
Comprehensive brand quality enhancement that systematically identifies improvement opportunities, implements strategic refinements, and maintains brand excellence through continuous optimization and evidence-based improvements.

## Usage Patterns
```bash
/brandforge improve [brand-element] --focus [area]
/brandforge improve --analysis --optimization --enhancement
/brandforge improve --refinement --polish --excellence
/brandforge improve --iterative --continuous --systematic
```

## Arguments
- **brand-element**: Specific brand component to improve
- **focus**: Improvement focus area or priority

## Improvement Focus Areas
- **strategy**: Strategic positioning and positioning refinement
- **creative**: Visual identity and creative asset enhancement
- **content**: Content quality and messaging improvement
- **experience**: Customer experience and touchpoint optimization
- **performance**: Brand performance and effectiveness improvement
- **consistency**: Brand consistency and standardization
- **accessibility**: Inclusive design and accessibility enhancement
- **engagement**: Customer engagement and interaction improvement

## Flags
- `--analysis`: Comprehensive brand analysis and assessment
- `--optimization`: Systematic optimization and improvement
- `--enhancement`: Quality enhancement and refinement
- `--refinement`: Detailed refinement and polishing
- `--iterative`: Iterative improvement cycles
- `--continuous`: Continuous improvement processes
- `--systematic`: Systematic improvement methodology
- `--evidence-based`: Evidence-driven improvement decisions
- `--metrics`: Performance metrics and measurement
- `--benchmarking**: Industry benchmarking and best practices
- `--feedback**: Customer feedback integration
- `--testing**: Improvement testing and validation

## Auto-Persona Activation
- **refactorer**: Systematic improvement and quality enhancement
- **performance**: Performance optimization and measurement
- **analyst**: Improvement analysis and insight generation
- **qa**: Quality assurance and validation

## MCP Server Integration
- **Sequential**: Multi-dimensional improvement analysis
- **Context7**: Quality improvement frameworks and methodologies
- **Playwright**: User experience testing and validation
