# /brandforge creative - Creative Development & Design

## Command Structure
```yaml
---
command: "/brandforge creative"
category: "Brand Creative Development"
purpose: "Visual identity, brand design, and creative asset development"
wave-enabled: true
performance-profile: "optimization"
---
```

## Purpose
Complete creative development encompassing visual identity, brand design systems, creative assets, and brand experiences. Creates cohesive brand aesthetics with strategic alignment to brand positioning.

## Usage Patterns
```bash
/brandforge creative [brand-name] --identity [style]
/brandforge creative --visual-system --design-guidelines
/brandforge creative --assets --templates --toolkit
/brandforge creative --experience --touchpoints --journey
```

## Arguments
- **brand-name**: Target brand for creative development
- **identity**: Visual identity style or approach

## Creative Styles
- **minimalist**: Clean, simple, sophisticated design
- **bold**: Strong, impactful, attention-grabbing design
- **playful**: Fun, approachable, energetic design
- **premium**: Luxury, high-end, sophisticated design
- **modern**: Contemporary, innovative, forward-thinking
- **classic**: Timeless, traditional, established design
- **tech**: Technology-focused, innovative, digital design
- **organic**: Natural, sustainable, approachable design

## Flags
- `--identity`: Visual identity system development
- `--logo`: Logo design and variations
- `--colors`: Color palette and usage guidelines
- `--typography`: Font systems and hierarchy
- `--imagery`: Photography and illustration style
- `--assets`: Creative asset development
- `--templates`: Brand template creation
- `--guidelines`: Brand guidelines documentation
- `--experience`: Brand experience design
- `--touchpoints`: Customer touchpoint design
- `--responsive`: Multi-device design systems
- `--accessibility`: Inclusive design standards

## Auto-Persona Activation
- **frontend**: Visual design and user experience focus
- **analyst**: Creative effectiveness and user testing
- **mentor**: Design education and best practices
- **scribe**: Design documentation and guidelines

## MCP Server Integration
- **Magic**: Visual brand components and design systems
- **Context7**: Design patterns and creative frameworks
- **Playwright**: Creative testing and user validation

## Creative Development Framework

### Visual Identity System
- **Logo Design**: Primary logo and variations
- **Color Palette**: Primary, secondary, and accent colors
- **Typography System**: Font hierarchy and usage
- **Iconography**: Icon sets and usage guidelines
- **Photography Style**: Image treatment and style
- **Illustration Style**: Custom illustration approach

### Brand Guidelines
- **Logo Usage**: Clear space, sizing, and placement
- **Color Usage**: Color combinations and accessibility
- **Typography**: Font pairings and hierarchy
- **Layout Systems**: Grid systems and composition
- **Brand Applications**: Real-world usage examples
- **Brand Voice**: Tone and communication style

### Creative Assets
- **Marketing Materials**: Business cards, brochures, flyers
- **Digital Assets**: Social media templates, email signatures
- **Packaging**: Product packaging and labels
- **Environmental**: Signage and physical branding
- **Merchandise**: Branded merchandise design
- **Presentations**: Branded presentation templates

### Brand Experience Design
- **Customer Journey**: Touchpoint mapping and optimization
- **User Experience**: Brand experience across channels
- **Interaction Design**: Digital interface design
- **Physical Experience**: Retail and physical space design
- **Service Design**: Service touchpoint design
- **Brand Storytelling**: Narrative and content design

## Quality Gates
- **Brand Consistency**: Cohesive visual identity across all touchpoints
- **Strategic Alignment**: Creative aligns with brand strategy and positioning
- **User Testing**: Creative effectiveness validated with target audience
- **Accessibility**: Design meets accessibility standards
- **Scalability**: Creative system scales across all applications
- **Brand Recognition**: Strong visual brand recognition and memorability

## Examples
```bash
# Complete visual identity development
/brandforge creative "Lumina Tech" --identity --logo --colors --typography

# Brand guideline creation
/brandforge creative --guidelines --templates --assets

# Brand experience design
/brandforge creative --experience --touchpoints --journey

# Responsive design system
/brandforge creative --responsive --accessibility --modern

# Creative asset development
/brandforge creative --assets --templates --toolkit --premium
```
