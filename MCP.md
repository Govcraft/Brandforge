# MCP.md - BrandForge Integration Specifications

BrandForge integration with market research, creative tools, and synthetic user systems for comprehensive brand strategy development.

## Server Selection Algorithm

**BrandForge Priority Matrix**:
1. **Brand Task-Server Affinity**: Match brand tasks to optimal servers based on capability matrix
2. **Research Rigor**: Server response time, data accuracy, research methodology compliance
3. **Creative Quality**: Design system integration, visual asset generation, brand consistency
4. **Market Intelligence**: Real-time market data, competitive intelligence, trend analysis
5. **User Research Excellence**: Synthetic user generation, validation protocols, ethical compliance

**Selection Process**: Brand Task Analysis → Server Capability Match → Research Quality Check → Creative Integration Assessment → Final Selection

## Context7 Integration (Market Research & Brand Patterns)

**Purpose**: Comprehensive market intelligence, competitive analysis, brand strategy patterns, cultural insights, and industry best practices

**Brand-Specific Activation Patterns**:
- **Automatic**: Brand strategy development, competitive analysis, market research, cultural positioning
- **Manual**: `--c7`, `--context7` flags
- **Smart**: Brand persona activation, strategic command execution

**Brand Strategy Workflows**:
1. **Market Analysis**: Industry trends, competitive landscapes, market sizing
2. **Brand Pattern Recognition**: Successful brand strategies across industries
3. **Cultural Intelligence**: Cultural insights for brand positioning and messaging
4. **Competitive Benchmarking**: Detailed competitive analysis and positioning gaps
5. **Best Practice Integration**: Proven brand development methodologies

**Integration Workflows**:
```yaml
Brand Market Analysis:
  1. Market Detection: Scan brand context, industry, target demographics
  2. Competitor Identification: Use `resolve-competitor-id` for Context7-compatible brands
  3. Intelligence Retrieval: `get-market-intelligence` with specific industry focus
  4. Pattern Extraction: Extract successful brand strategies and positioning approaches
  5. Strategy Implementation: Apply patterns with proper attribution and cultural adaptation
  6. Validation: Verify strategy against market data and competitive landscape
  7. Caching: Store successful brand patterns for session reuse
```

**Brand-Specific Error Recovery**:
- Market data unavailable → WebSearch for alternative sources → Manual market analysis
- Cultural insights timeout → Use cached cultural knowledge → Note limitations
- Competitive data gaps → Primary research → Expert consultation
- Industry mismatch → Broader market analysis → Cross-industry pattern recognition
